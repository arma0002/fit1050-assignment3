Asked questions

Frequently Asked Questions

  • All our stripe kits are custom designed and not a copy of any genuine decal kit. We do this to stay safe from copyright issues. When creating a kit we start from scratch. Once designed, the kit is made and fitted in shop. We are constantly trying to create new designs for all vehicles. If you are local to South East QLD and your style of car does not have a kit listed by us, please contact as we would be interested in creating and fitting a custom kit for you.

  • Please ensure you thoroughly read the item description and confirm that it matches your vehicle. Verify that your address information is accurate and up to date. We cannot accept responsibility for incorrectly fitted decals. All sales are final, and refunds are not provided for changes of mind or incorrect installation. Re-posting charges will apply if items are returned due to incorrect postal information or uncollected items from the post office. Payment must be made immediately after purchase; we accept PayPal or bank transfer as our only payment methods due to their trusted and secure nature.

  • Most of our kits are made to order and posted the same day of order (depending on purchase time of day). All items are posted with Australia Post and can be tracked regularly. Because we are not open on weekends no orders will be posted. Our local Australia post store is closed on Saturday and Sunday, so orders will be posted on Monday. Please ensure your postage details are correct and up to date.

  • Fitting stripe kits is not easy for everyone. Professionals charge $150 plus an hour for fitting stripe kits. It does take a certain amount of time to develop these skills in quality as does every trade. However, most people will do it themselves, and we have provided an installation guide to assist you with your fitting. We also recommend watching YouTube videos on stripe application, both for large and small decals. If you are still unsure about fitting them, we suggest going to a local signwriter or your local panel shop, as they will have someone on staff or know someone who can fit them for you.

    Please ensure the surface is cleaned properly to avoid stripes lifting or unwanted grit beneath vinyl. Most of our kits will have excess stripe at one or both ends for easier application. For example, the front driver's door may have a longer stripe than the door itself, so when you place it on the door, you only have to line it up horizontally, with the ends overhanging. Once you have squeegeed the decal on, carefully remove the application tape, and then, using a new razor blade (which we do not provide), carefully cut excess vinyl from each end. Repeat the process for the rear door, and then you are done.

  • If for some reason an item needs to be returned, you will need to take photos of the item. Showing clearly the damaged product (or whatever the issue is) but photo evidence and communication with us is required for return or refund.
